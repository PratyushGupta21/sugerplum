# Refund Policy - Sugar Plum Bakery

**Last Updated: March 29, 2026**

## 1. Overview

At Sugar Plum Bakery, we strive to provide the highest quality baked goods and excellent customer service. This Refund Policy outlines our procedures for returns, exchanges, and refunds.

## 2. Perishable Food Items

### 2.1 No Returns on Food Items
Due to the perishable nature of our baked goods, we do not accept returns or exchanges of food items once they have been delivered or picked up.

### 2.2 Exceptions
In rare cases, we may offer a refund or credit for food items if:
- The product was damaged during delivery
- The wrong item was delivered
- The product was clearly defective upon receipt

### 2.3 Time Frame
Any issues with food items must be reported within 2 hours of delivery or pickup.

## 3. Non-Food Items

### 3.1 Return Period
Non-food items (such as merchandise, packaging, or accessories) may be returned within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

### 3.2 Condition Requirements
Items must be:
- In original, unused condition
- In original packaging
- Accompanied by proof of purchase

### 3.3 Non-Returnable Items
The following non-food items are not eligible for return:
- Custom or personalized items
- Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
- Items without original packaging

## 4. Refund Process

### 4.1 Refund Methods
Refunds will be processed using the original payment method:
- Credit/debit cards: 3-5 business days
- Digital wallets: 1-3 business days
- Cash payments: Store credit or check

### 4.2 Processing Time
- Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after approval
- You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed

### 4.3 Shipping Costs
- Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error
- We will refund shipping costs if the return is due to our mistake

## 5. Order Cancellations

### 5.1 Before Processing
Orders can be cancelled before they begin processing. Full refunds will be issued.

### 5.2 During Processing
Once an order begins processing (baking has started), cancellations may result in partial refunds or store credit.

### 5.3 After Delivery
Post-delivery cancellations are not accepted for food items.

## 6. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

### 6.1 Reporting Issues
If you receive a damaged or incorrect order:
- Take photos of the items and packaging
- Contact us immediately at [phone] or [email]
- Provide your order number and description of the issue

### 6.2 Resolution
We will:
- Replace the damaged/incorrect items at no additional cost
- Issue a full refund if replacement is not possible
- Provide delivery credits for future orders

## 7. Quality Guarantee

### 7.1 Freshness Guarantee
We guarantee that all items are fresh and baked on the day of delivery or pickup.

### 7.2 Satisfaction Guarantee
If you are not completely satisfied with your order, please contact us within 2 hours, and we will work with you to make it right.

## 8. Special Circumstances

### 8.1 Allergies and Dietary Restrictions
If an order does not meet your specified dietary needs due to our error, we will provide a full refund or replacement.

### 8.2 Delivery Issues
If delivery is significantly delayed (more than 2 hours past the estimated time), we may offer partial refunds or delivery credits.

### 8.3 Force Majeure
We are not responsible for delays or issues caused by events beyond our control (weather, traffic, etc.).

## 9. Gift Cards and Store Credit

### 9.1 Gift Cards
Gift cards are non-refundable and do not expire.

### 9.2 Store Credit
Store credit may be issued in certain circumstances and is valid for 12 months from the date of issue.
